How to Use

This companion is built around one idea: small, honest daily action adds up to extraordinary results.

1

Start with your non-negotiable

Every day, write the ONE thing you will absolutely do — no matter what. It's not your biggest goal, it's your minimum commitment to yourself.

2

Tasks are ordered easiest first — on purpose

The first few tasks are already filled in and simple. Tick these first — those early wins create real momentum for the harder ones below them.

3

Health · Digital Detox · Night Routine

Three sections track the habits that compound quietly over 90 days. The night routine especially — preparing tomorrow the night before is one of the highest-ROI habits you can build.

4

End with the honesty check

Every day ends with one question: did I do what I told myself I would do? This isn't judgment — it's data.

"The goal isn't perfection. It's becoming someone who keeps their word — to themselves."

— the only rule of this challenge

Grace Days

Some days you just can't do it all — that's allowed. You get 3 Grace Days across the whole 90. Use one from the Today screen when you need it; it still moves you forward and it's always logged openly, not hidden.

Keep this on your phone

Tap your browser's Share button (or menu, top right) and choose "Add to Home Screen." It'll sit on your phone like any other app, and works even offline.
